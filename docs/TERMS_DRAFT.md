# Crewith Terms of Service Draft

This is a product planning draft and must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public release.

## 1. Service

Crewith provides tools for clubs and groups to manage members, fees, schedules, attendance, notices, join requests, invites, and reminders.

## 2. Users

Crewith has three default club roles:

- Owner
- Operator
- Member

Role names may be customized later, but permissions are based on the role assigned inside each club.

## 3. Club Operations

Club owners and operators are responsible for:

- Inviting or approving members
- Managing member status
- Recording fee payment status accurately
- Creating schedules and notices
- Managing attendance records
- Using member information only for legitimate club operation purposes

## 4. Member Responsibilities

Members are responsible for:

- Providing accurate phone number and profile information
- Responding to schedules honestly
- Reviewing club notices
- Following club-specific rules set by the owner or operators

## 5. Payments

The MVP excludes in-app payment processing. Crewith records fee status entered by operators but does not process payments directly until payment integration is added.

## 6. Notifications

Crewith may send app push notifications for:

- Fee reminders
- Schedule reminders
- Notice reminders
- Join request or invite updates

Users may disable push notifications through app or device settings.

## 7. Restrictions

Users must not:

- Access club data without authorization
- Misuse another user's phone number or account
- Upload unlawful or harmful content
- Use the service to send spam or abusive messages
- Attempt to bypass role-based permissions

## 8. Service Changes

Crewith may change, add, or remove features during MVP development. Important changes should be communicated to users or club operators.

## 9. Termination

Users may leave a club. Owners or operators may remove a member according to club rules. Personal profile data should be deleted after the configured retention period, while fee and attendance records may be retained as operational history.

## 10. Contact

Service contact information must be added before public launch.
