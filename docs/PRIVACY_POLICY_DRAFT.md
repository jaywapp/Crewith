# Crewith Privacy Policy Draft

This is a product planning draft and must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public release.

## 1. Collected Information

Crewith may collect the following information to provide club management features:

- Name
- Mobile phone number
- Profile image
- Club membership role and status
- Fee payment status
- Event response and attendance records
- Notice read, comment, and reaction records
- Join request greeting
- Device push token

## 2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for:

- Phone number authentication
- Club member list management
- Fee payment tracking
- Event attendance management
- Notice delivery and read tracking
- Join request and invite management
- App push notifications
- Customer support and service reliability

## 3. Retention

- Active club membership data is retained while the member belongs to the club.
- Personal profile data for left or removed members is scheduled for deletion after 30 days.
- Fee and attendance history may be retained as club operation records after personal profile fields are removed or anonymized.
- Authentication and device records are retained as needed for account access and notification delivery.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Crewith may use:

- Firebase Authentication for phone authentication
- Cloud Firestore for service data
- Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications
- Firebase Storage for profile images
- Naver Maps for location search and map display

## 5. User Rights

Users may request:

- Access to their personal information
- Correction of inaccurate information
- Deletion of personal profile information
- Withdrawal from a club
- Disabling push notifications

## 6. Security

Crewith should protect personal information through:

- Role-based access control
- Authenticated API access
- Environment variable secret management
- Limited operator access to club-scoped data
- Scheduled deletion of personal profile data after withdrawal or removal

Privacy contact information must be added before public launch.
